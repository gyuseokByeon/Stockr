About Stockr

Stockr is an advanced web application which allows for the tracking and prediction of shares on the stock market. The ability for an individual to accurately predict the future changes in the price of a stock proves very advantageous, as it allows for a higher return of investment, and significantly reduces the risk associated with trading.

Trading requires a quick response to changes; with access to forecasted prices, shares can be bought or sold in anticipation of a fluctuation before other competitors are able to do so. The aim of all this being to alleviate some of the guess work which is required to work in this sector.

Through industry leading machine learning algorithms, Stockr offers accurate next day forecasts which allow brokers to make educated decisions and when to buy and sell, enabling them to thrive on the stock exchange.
